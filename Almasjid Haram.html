Masjid Al-Haram

Al-Masjid Al-Haram | The Biggest Mosque on The World The mosque that stands majestically in the city of Mecca is the largest mosque in the world. Standing on an area of 598,657 m2. Grand Mosque can accommodate more than 900,000 pilgrims from around the world. The mosque was built in 638 AD when the reign of Umar bin Khattab is regarded as the holiest shrine for Muslims because in it there is the Ka'ba Qibla direction throughout the Muslim world as well as the main purpose of worship Hajj.
